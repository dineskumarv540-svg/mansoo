# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, or religion.

## Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
