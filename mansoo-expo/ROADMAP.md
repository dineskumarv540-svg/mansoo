# 🗺️ Mansoo Strategic Product Roadmap (2026 – 2027)

This document outlines the planned feature release trajectory for **Mansoo – The Voice of Heart**.

---

## 📍 Q3 2026 — Foundational Platform Excellence (Current Release v1.0)
- [x] Complete 4px Grid Design System & Atomic Component Library (`src/components/ui/`).
- [x] Feature 1: Live Writing Rooms (Twitter Spaces + Discord style writing circles).
- [x] Enterprise Security & Security Rules hardening.
- [x] OpenAPI 3.1 Specification & Customer Support Ticket Ecosystem.

---

## ⚔️ Q4 2026 — Community Engagement & Audio Capabilities
- [ ] **Feature 2: Writing Battles**: 1v1 live writing duels, anonymous audience voting, AI spam prevention, and weekly tournaments.
- [ ] **Feature 3: Anonymous Writing**: Identity-secured anonymous posting, comments, polls, and stories.
- [ ] **Feature 4: Voice Poetry**: Native audio recording, background music mixing, waveform visualization, and audio publishing (MP3, M4A, WAV).

---

## 📚 Q1 2027 — Publishing & Monetization Ecosystem
- [ ] **Feature 5: Digital Book Builder**: Cover designer, chapter layout, EPUB/PDF export, and 3D page-turning preview.
- [ ] **Feature 6: Mansoo Monthly Magazine**: Automated editorial compilation, top poem selections, and PDF downloads.
- [ ] **Feature 7: Creator Economy**: Tip Jar, paid subscriber posts, membership tiers, and Razorpay/UPI payout dashboard.

---

## 🤖 Q2 2027 — AI & Algorithmic Recommendation Engine
- [ ] **Feature 8: AI Writing Assistant**: Smart title suggestions, grammar check, tone analysis, and readability scoring.
- [ ] **Feature 11: Personalization Engine**: ML recommendation model based on reading history, category affinity, and cold-start onboarding.
- [ ] **Feature 12: Professional Writer Portfolio**: Printable PDF portfolio resume, custom `mansoo.in/@username` web URLs, and verified writer badges.
